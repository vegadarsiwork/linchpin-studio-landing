Policies

Clear rules. No surprises.

The fine print — clear, upfront, and built to protect both sides.

01

Minimum reel order

Minimum 3 reels — no singles. Base ₹1,500/reel (Solo, Gold).

02

Influencer allocation

≤5 reels: 1 influencer. 10 reels: 2. Extra: ₹2,500–₹6,000.

03

Premium influencers

Minimum 3-reel commitment. Affects per-reel pricing.

04

Revision policy

2 free revisions per reel. Extra revisions charged.

05

Shoot day limits

5-reel: max 1–2 days. 10-reel: max 3 days. Extra = fees.

06

Raw footage charges

+40% of total bill. Delivered after full payment.

07

Influencer pricing variance

₹2,500–₹6,000 per influencer based on profile & reach.

08

Custom bulk

10+ reels: case-by-case. Includes dedicated project manager.
